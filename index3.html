Proposition for a fiction airline company (Calico Air)

This project was all about creating a proposition for a potential redesign of a fictional airline's website where we had to first understand and write about what users wants and needs in an airline website for processes such as booking their flights and generally when they are looking and using a website. Then we took this knowledge and applied it to wireframes and through commentary on specific techniques ad principes we found out and used, we explained how this website would be attractive and efficient for user experience.

Improving Engoo's "language test"

This project was a trimester long project which had three parts to it. We were working on a real-life project with a real client whos name was Luke McCrohon who is the CTO of an online english learning platfrom called "Engoo". Luke communicted to our class that there were several UX problems with the website and that if we had any potential solutions as students that could be appreciated. This was our project and we had to pick an aspect of the website to focus on for the project.

Part 1 - Whats not working?

For the first part of the project we had to find a specific area of the website out of the several that needed attention and we had to investigate and research through primary and secondary means as to what were the problems, causes and effects in terms of the wider picture in relation to the website. I picked the area of the tutor hiring process which essentially is like applying to a job on a job website and I did this my conducting interviews, making graphs, and also writing up a brief for potential solutions in my next step for the project.




Part 2 - Fixing it!

In the second part of my project we had to deliver on our brief and adress the changes/improvements we were going to make in the spcific area of the website (Engoo) we were focussing on, designing a new prototype for the area. We could wither have done fast iterative lower fidelity wireframing with small bits of testing or we could do a couple high fidelity tests with one big tests which i decided to do as my topic of the emotion on the language test woud benefit one large test with participants telling me how they feel/thoughts rather than short wireframed tests which would be useless.


Summarised presentation (5 minute presentation)

Part 3 - Presenting to a group who dont know what Engoo is!

The last part of the project wasnt as big as all we had to do was prsent our work again but this time, we had to pretend as if we are communicating an idea to a group of people that have no idea what Engoo actually is and through this, communicate our project and improvements. We had a limit of 5 minutes for our videos and because it wasnt much time, we had to only communicate the important points to increase understanding for unknowlegedable people.
